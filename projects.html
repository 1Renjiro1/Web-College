About Me
Story

In a galaxy far away, galactic control fight aliens to save the universe from doom. A war fought throughout the years. It is now your time to fight. It is your job to save the universe from the evil aliens and claim the universe in humanities name. This story is very simple and not overcomplicated this is because of the target audience and the right length to keep people of the age group engaged with the story and game. It also sounds like Star Wars which is commonly associated as one of the biggest space movies. My main scene will be set in space with orbiting planets to add life to the scene which can apply to the target audience as it would grab their attention rather than having them as stationary. The time-period is going to be set in the distant future as I want it to have that futuristic feel to it.

Requirements

My requirement for the game is to make a fully working third person space shooter with a balanced controller for the spaceship. The target audience for the game is 5 or over this means that I will need to target my advertising towards the parents of the child therefore, bright colours will be a good idea as it is more appealing towards that age group as it shows it is child friendly. I am also targeting this game more towards PC this is because it will be easier to understand how the game works and its functionality compared to if it was on console or PC.

Game Goals

The core gameplay is to destroy enemies that will spawn in waves and target the player for which they must destroy and get points in the game the more powerful the enemies are the more points you will claim. The rewards have no purpose other than to give the player feedback. I made my game goals this way because I want it to be simple for my target audience.

Game Mechanics

The key game mechanics is that enemies will spawn in waves and target the player. The player actions will have a custom-made controller for the spaceship which is inspired by the YouTuber Kyle Rasmusson UE4 Scripting Jet Movement however, I am planning to make tweaks to it to give it a more fun interaction. The player will also use mouse button 1 to shoot the bullet this once again will have to be made from scratch but inspired by Royal Skies How to Make Bullets.
